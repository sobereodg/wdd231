Afrobeats Music Discovery - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Afrobeats Music Discovery

Reason: This name reflects the site's focus on exploring and promoting the Afrobeats music genre, helping fans discover songs, artists, and trends.

Site Purpose

The site aims to connect fans to Afrobeats music through artist spotlights, trending tracks, curated playlists, concert news, and album releases.

Scenarios

  • Which new Afrobeats songs are trending this month?
  • Where can I find playlists featuring upcoming Afrobeats artists?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #D90429 - Used for headers and call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #EDF2F4 - Used for background and contrast text areas.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - Used for all headings.
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif - Used for body text.
